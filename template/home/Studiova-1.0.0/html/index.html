Frequently asked questions

EcoCoin is a cryptocurrency that rewards users for engaging in eco-friendly activities. Our goal is to create a global community of individuals who are passionate about protecting the environment.

You can earn EcoCoins by completing a variety of eco-friendly activities, such as planting trees, cycling, and recycling. Simply upload a photo or video of yourself completing the activity to our platform, and you will be rewarded with EcoCoins.

You can use your EcoCoins to purchase products and services from our partners, or you can trade them on a cryptocurrency exchange.

To get started, simply create an account on our platform. Once you have created an account, you can start completing eco-friendly activities and earning EcoCoins.
